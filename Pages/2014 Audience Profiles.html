Mental Health in the Technology Start-Up Industry

Average Profile


The ages of the subjects largely ranged from 27 to 36 years old, therefore suggesting prior experience, whether it be in Technology or not. Almost two-thirds (61%) of the respondents came from the United States. Of these, the single largest group came from the West Coast. A potential driver of mental health issue is the size of the company, which may be proxied by the number of co-workers. Most of the respondents belonged to companies with employment in the 5-25 or the 25-100 range. This shows that the majority of the companies represented are small by definition.


Male Profile

The pie chart that visualizes the country distribution shows that the number of male participants from the United Kingdom doubles from female employees working in the United Kingdom. Also, the number of male participants from countries other than United States, United Kingdom, and Canada double from that of female participants. Other key observations show that the least number of male participants work in the east coast, while the most work in the west coast.


Female Profile

Female participants were typically younger and closer in age than in the male dataset. They are also more likely to work in United States as well. When it came to the coast distribution, the trends showed that most of the participants in the east coast were female.
